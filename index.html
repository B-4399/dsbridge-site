Student Challenges

PCC Legalization

Here is Akash an Indian national who was residing in the UAE. He got an enrollment into a Lithuanian University and followed every procedures, submitted his digital copies for TRP evaluation, got an appointment date at the UAE external service provider. His PCC needed an extra stamp from the UAE consulate in Riga-Latvia as stated by MOFA Lithuania and he knew of no one in Europe who could take his PCC to Latvia for an extra stamp. With just 4 days to his appointment date, Akash got anxious but was later helped by a Nepali lady who happened to have had a similar problem but had friend in Europe who could help with her PCC.Akash got his PCC from Lithuania in the morning on the day of his appointment date. DsBRIDGE question is, what if there wasn't a Nepali to help Akash out?.

Health Insurance

Kofi from Ghana got his admission letter from his University, had finished with all other formalities only the health insurance plan got him stucked. He didn't know where and how to get an insurance plan that will be valid and meets migris' standards. His University of admission couldn't answer some of his doubts and questions such as: *Insurance duration, *what does the insurance covers and *The price. he endsup getting an insurance plan that isn't valid in Lithuania and got his TRP rejected for the same reason. It was during his second application that he got a positive decision because DsBRIDGE bridged that gap.

Documents for TRP application

  • Health insurance meeting migris standards
  • Bank statement of atleast 6 months of transactions
  • Police clearance Certificate legalized properly
  • Admission letter from the university(for first time students)
  • Mediation number from University
  • Passport copy with atleast 2years validity
