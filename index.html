Our Core Services

PCC Legalization

Fast and reliable legalizaion of PCC at student's specific home embassy in the EU as stated on the official website of ministry of foreign affairs lithuania. our service fee is 100 euro, then the student pays for transportation and their embassy charged fee

Community bulding

Health Insurance

Affordable and approved health insurance plans for international students

Airport Pickup

Airport pickup for arriving students in Lithuania to their hotel or accomodation.

Community building

  • Sharing common solutions to challenges faced by students during TRP apllication or renewals.
  • A community where students can carryout their research projects and thesis information among their peers

Documents for TRP application

  • Health insurance meeting migris standards
  • Bank statement of atleast 6 months of transactions
  • Police clearance Certificate legalized properly
  • Admission letter from the university(for first time students)
  • Mediation number from University
  • Passport copy with atleast 2years validity

About DsBRIDGE

DsBRIDGE is a student-first friendly hub in Lithuania dedicated to solving common challenges faced by international students. We understand the struggles because we have lived them. Our mission is to create smooth pathway for students settling in Lithuania. We are reliable, fast in delivery. Moreso,it is a community where students can carry out research projects and thesis information We also search for partnerships with companies who offers internship programs, volunteering opportunity, or workshop learning programs where our community members who meets the companies programs requiremnts can strengthen their professional skills. We carry out quaterly assessments to the know our different segments of students and their challenges.
